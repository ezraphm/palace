# Social Farming — Chapter 11: Better Together

**Start:** August 4, 2025 | **Runs:** 3 months
**Status:** Currently running (as of May 2026)

## Core Mechanic: Cheer Tokens

- Each day receive **3 free Cheer tokens** to gift other players
- Cheers help others with major projects:
  - ✨ **Build a Monument** — grants powerful social buffs
  - 🍲 **Cook a Meal** — huge XP bonuses
  - 🍉 **Grow a Giant Fruit** — harvest and sell for Love Charms

> Only with multiple friends can you complete these social projects!

## Friends System Upgrade

- **Improved Discovery UI**: see player stats, FLOWER value
- **Follow Friends**: stay updated with favorite farmers
- **Messaging**: send cheers and messages directly

## The Great Cleanup 🧹

Visit friends' farms daily to help tidy up:
- Clean **Garbage**
- Pull **Weeds**
- Clear **Dung**
- Craft a **Net** for **Pests**

Each cleanup helps your friend climb the **Discovery Leaderboard**. You earn:
- Coins
- Cheers
- Love Charms
- Cleanup items can be exchanged for more Cheer tokens

## Crafting Box Updates

1. **Instant Crafting**: use Gems to speed up
2. **Simplified UI**: small cosmetics moved to Wardrobe or Landscaping Shop
3. **Bumpkin NPC Recipes**: discover new recipes by befriending NPCs
4. **Doll Crafting**

## Dolls 🪆 — Ultimate Collectible

- Craftable at Crafting Box
- **24 designs** total, including 8 from Community Design Contest
- Requires experimentation — combine materials to discover more
- Uses: 5 Weekly Bounties + Deliveries

## Love Charms

- Earned through social in-game activities
- Used in special shops and exchanges
