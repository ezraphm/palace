**Status:** Coming in the chapter *after* Salt & Spices (estimated mid-2026)

## How It Works

- Resets player level BUT does **NOT** reset other progression
- Players **prestige to new islands**: Swamp, Spooky, Fairy, Gold Island
- Start at high tile size — no space limitation
- Brings back expansion nodes called **"drip rate"**
- Estimated **3 years to fully complete** (P5 level)

## Ascension Shards

- New resource earned through prestige
- Spent on:
  - **Tier 4 skills** (beyond current skill tree)
  - **Ascension Shop items**
- Shop may include powerful automation:
  - Crop Machine Mark Two
  - Auto planter
- Potential reduced sales tax for prestige players

## Impact on Existing Systems

- **Replaces Sunstones system** — Sunstones deprecated
- Obsidian exchange simplified (cut out sunstones middle step)

## Island Progression

Basic → Petal Paradise → Desert → Volcano → **Swamp** (next!) → Spooky → Fairy → Gold Island
