**Introduced:** February 2026 (post-Chapters overhaul)

## Chapter Dashboard

- New icon in top left for unified overview
- Replaces old interfaces
- Mobile-friendly
- Includes:
  - **Leaderboard**
  - **Collection progress** (e.g., 2/30 tracked)
  - **Tracks** (Battle Pass)
  - **Raffles**
  - **Daily/Social rewards**
  - Collection tab includes mutants, track/shop/auction items
- Bonus prize in chapter shed for full collection

## Tracks (Battle Pass Style)

- Replaces the old chapter store
- Earn rewards by completing
- Quick to finish if doing all activities
- Visible in dashboard
- Makes chapter content more accessible to new/casual players

## Raffles

- High engagement: 5000+ participants per week
- 100 prizes; top 1-2 on-chain prizes manually airdropped
- Good **ticket sink** for players outside top 500-1000
- Strategy: split tickets across raffles for better odds (one win per raffle max)
- Security prevents suspicious users (bots) from winning valuables

## Post-Launch Fixes

- **Megastore chests**: reduced excessive top-end item drops
- **Dollorcaris maps**: changed fish (orfish → football fish)
- **Pet Aura costs**: increased base gem cost for pet food requests
- Chapter points shown on animal bounties
- Tweaked chapter shop mega item prices
